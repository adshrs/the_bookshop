The Effects of Technology in our daily lives

Technology has influenced people around the globe. As technology has developed it has given new lease of life to humankind. Considering the prior life of humans, the development of technology has brought a vast change in our lives being highly significant in today’s era. From the barbarian era to the modern era, human civilization has evolved profoundly in the context of technology. Over many years, the rapid progress of technology has definitely made our life faster and more convenient.

21st century, the era of science and technology has given rise to the digital world. One of the greatest innovations of science and technology is the internet. It has provided a wide range of services. Services like instant messaging, sharing information, and accessing various information. Similarly, inventions like cell phones and other high-tech gear have taken over today’s world. From carrying out our daily life activities to learning we people have been using these technologies to the fullest. Many applications which are used for learning are highly effective. It has not only made education easier but also entertaining; somehow colorful. It can be considered a source of entertainment as well.

Although development in science and technology have brought us many advantages, we cannot omit the association of it with negative impacts. We can witness that these days, technologies have been cramping the social development of children. We see them so indulged in their devices. The Internet itself is a small word but the environment it has created is huge. And within this environment, there reside many people. We must always draw a line to be safe from its detrimental effects. In conclusion, technology has many beneficial effects when is used wisely. Contrary to this we cannot alter the outcome. It makes our life pleasant and safer if we utilize it properly. Technology is indeed the greatest boon to mankind.
